# Goals — Jul 20 to Oct 17, 2026 (90 days)

Each goal has a metric so "how am I doing" has an actual answer, not a feeling.

## 1. Scripture
- **Target:** Read the whole Bible (1,189 chapters) by Oct 17.
- **Pace:** ~13 chapters/day average, tracked weekly not daily (see
  `bible.md`) so one heavy day doesn't feel like failure.
- **Metric:** chapters read ÷ 1,189.

## 2. Prayer
- **Target:** 2.5 hrs/day, 7 days/week (church + pastoral days included).
- **Metric:** minutes logged/day vs 150 target; weekly average.

## 3. Exercise
- **Target:** 6 days/week, Thursday (Sabbath) off.
- **Metric:** sessions/week vs 6; type + duration logged.

## 4. Body composition
- **Target:** 82–84kg → 75kg, leaner, reduced belly fat, by Oct 17.
- **Pace:** ~0.5–0.7kg/week — sustainable, not aggressive.
- **Metric:** weekly weigh-in (same day/time), trend not daily noise.
  Optional: waist measurement every 2 weeks as the "leaner" proxy, since
  scale weight alone won't show belly fat specifically.

## 5. Eating
- **Target:** consistently whole-food, protein-forward meals; organic
  where practical, not a hard requirement (see `food-notes.md`).
- **Metric:** qualitative daily log (on-plan / off-plan) rather than
  calorie counting, unless you want to add numbers later.

## 6. Business — both organizations
- **Target:** definite, tracked outcomes per week per org — not just hours
  spent. Defined per-org in `business.md`, including what you
  will and won't delegate.
- **Metric:** weekly outcomes hit ÷ weekly outcomes planned.

## 7. Learning
- **Target:** 3 self-development books finished by Oct 17; ongoing
  learning across tech, admin, management, health, structures, science,
  and global affairs.
- **Metric:** books finished; learning log entries/week (`learning.md`).

## 8. Socialization
- **Target:** dedicated social time weekly — not incidental, actually
  scheduled.
- **Metric:** social blocks/week logged.

## 9. Sleep
- **Target:** consistent, sufficient sleep — set a target bed/wake window
  once you know your ideal hours, then hold it.
- **Metric:** hours/night + subjective quality (1–5) logged.

## Supporting habits (not goals themselves, but what makes the above work)
- **Daily 30-min "do nothing" block** — unstructured, no input, no
  agenda. Protects against burnout eating into the goals above.
- **Weekly review** (Sundays) and **monthly review** (~every 30 days) —
  the habit of reviewing your own output, which you asked for directly.
- **Weekly Sabbath (Thursday)** — full rest from work and exercise.
